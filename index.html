Trashpicker

Welcome to the training area! Click on the examples below to learn where each type of trash should be thrown away. Then, when you're ready to put your knowledge to the test , scroll to the bottom of the screen, and click "I'm Ready to Play". Controls: Hold "shift" while within range of a piece of trash to pick it up. Move with directional arrows while continuing to hold "shift" to carry the trash to the trashbin where it belongs. When you're within range of the trashbin, your cursor will glow blue! Release "shift" to throw away the trash. When all pieces of trash have been thrown away, the game is over!
